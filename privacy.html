🦆 DuckDash Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

Thank you for playing DuckDash. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DuckDash handles your information.

Information We Collect

DuckDash does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Game Data

Your game progress, including bread count and upgrades, is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device.

Internet Connection

DuckDash does not require an internet connection to play.

Advertising

DuckDash does not display advertisements.

Analytics

DuckDash does not use analytics or tracking services.

Third-Party Services

DuckDash does not integrate any third-party services that collect personal information.

Children's Privacy

DuckDash is designed to be suitable for players of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be published on this page.
